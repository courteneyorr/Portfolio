FS 24/7

This is a design for a website called FS 24/7. My client wanted a simple, professional site that was easy for users to understand and navigate. I chose the blue and white color palette for its simplicity.

K&R Software

I designed this website for a company called K&R Software. My client asked to incorporate the red color from his logo throughout the home page. The palette is offset by gray and black, with a slider that advertises what the company has to offer.

SafeTime

This is a website design for a product that protects women from attackers through the use of a "safe button" attached to her keychain. My client had already chosen her palette, and wanted me to incorporate it throughtout the site. She asked for a "fun, friendly site that invites women to learn more about her product."

Music Poster

This design is for a school project that asked us to create a poster for our favorite band. I chose bright, primary colors to fit in with the band's image, and went with an 80's theme to match the decade that the band was most popular.

Jarts

I designed this web advertisement for a school project that asked us to recreate a current ad for the vintage product, "Jarts." Instead of excluding the dangers of the lawn dart, I chose to advertise the danger as "fun."

Lionfish Logo

This is a design for a school project that asked us to create a logo based on the Lionfish. I chose to create a logo for a film company, and created an origami fish made out of film with the shadow of a lionfish behind it.

Forrest Gump

Created using a micron pen, I made this piece for a project that asked us to create a storyboard from a movie assigned to us. I was assigned Forrest Gump, and chose to recreate the scene where Bubba was injured during the Vietnam War.
